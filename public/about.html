How Finlytte uses AI

Finlytte combines modern AI with financial data to deliver clear stock analysis. For each stock we:

  • Gather financial statements and normalize key metrics
  • Compute important ratios and trends
  • Use AI to interpret results, produce a risk meter, and compare peers
  • Return a concise, plain-language summary for investors

This tool is for educational use only and is not investment advice.
